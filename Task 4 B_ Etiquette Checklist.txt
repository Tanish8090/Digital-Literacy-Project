PART B


Social Media Do’s :
1.Use a professional profile picture.
2.Think before posting any content on social media.
3.Maintain a positive tone while using social media.
4.Keep your information private.
5.Use strong and secure passwords.
6.Verify information before sharing it with others.
7.Use polite language while interacting with other people on social media.
8.Give credit to the original creator while sharing information or content with others.
9.Keep your privacy settings updated.
10.Share content that is meaningful and valuable.
11.Be mindful of your audience while using social media.
12.Use social media for learning purposes.
13.Report inappropriate content to the authorities.
14.Maintain a balance between your real life and online life.
15.Follow the rules and regulations of the particular community or website.


Social Media Don’ts:
1.Don’t share your personal information with people on social media.
2.Don’t post offensive or abusive content on social media.
3.Don’t spread false news or information on social media.
4.Don’t fight or argue with people on social media.
5.Don’t share every single thing about your life with people on social media.
6.Don’t use weak or common passwords while creating an account on social media.
7.Don’t post content or information without permission from the original creator.
8.Don’t ignore your privacy settings while using social media.
9.Don’t post content or information when you are feeling angry or emotional.
10.Don’t follow unknown links or messages sent by unknown people.
11.Don’t bully or troll people on social media.
12.Don’t overuse or abuse social media.
13.Don’t post illegal or inappropriate content on social media.
14.Don’t believe everything that is shown or told on social media.
15.Don’t harm your reputation by posting inappropriate content or information on social media.
